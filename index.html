Concept

Japanese precision, Mediterranean light.

Kasamatsu brings the restraint of Japanese dining to the rhythm of the coast: clean flavors, quiet craft, and seasonal ingredients shaped by the markets and waters around Saint-Tropez.

Near Ramatuelle, the restaurant takes its name from the umbrella pine: a tree of shade, breeze, and composed generosity.
